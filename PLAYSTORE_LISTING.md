## Full Description (4000 chars max)

```
Learn Hindi the fun way — no internet required!

Seekho Hindi is a free offline Hindi learning app that teaches you vocabulary through smart flashcards, quizzes, streaks, and a full gamification system. Whether you're a complete beginner or brushing up on advanced words, Seekho Hindi adapts to your level.

📚 WHAT YOU'LL LEARN
• 700+ Hindi words across 6 difficulty levels
• Alphabet (Devanagari script, matras, conjuncts)
• Grammar essentials (pronouns, verbs, postpositions, question words)
• Beginner to Expert vocabulary
• Common phrases and cultural context

🎮 GAMIFICATION THAT KEEPS YOU GOING
• Earn XP and level up as you learn
• Daily and weekly quests to stay motivated
• Unlock badges — Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary
• Maintain daily streaks and track your longest run
• Leaderboard to track your own progress over time

📊 SMART LEARNING SYSTEM
• Spaced Repetition (SM-2 algorithm) — review words just before you forget them
• AI Adaptive Learning — harder words appear more often
• Accuracy, retention rate, and learning velocity tracking
• Detailed analytics dashboard

✅ FEATURES
• Completely offline — learn without internet
• Dark mode support
• Text-to-speech pronunciation for every word
• 6 difficulty levels + Alphabet and Grammar decks
• Quiz mode with 30 questions per session
• Interactive flashcards with flip animation
• Daily streak tracking with milestone celebrations
• Error boundary — the app never crashes silently
• Explore tab — common phrases, grammar tips, cultural notes

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
All your data stays on your device. No ads. No tracking. No analytics sent to servers.

Perfect for:
• Students learning Hindi for school or university
• Travelers visiting India
• Heritage speakers reconnecting with their roots
• Professionals working with Hindi-speaking teams
• Anyone curious about one of the world's most spoken languages

Start your Hindi journey today — नमस्ते!
```
